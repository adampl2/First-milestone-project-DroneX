The future of Logistics

Building Sustainable Future

At DroneX, our goal is to take your delivery service to the next level. We do this by sharing our drones and technology with you. After filling the form, we will get back to you with further instructions within a week. You will receive a tablet and you're good to go!

Our drones had been programmed to deliver goods of up to 12kg with the utmost precision. With DroneX you don't have to worry about damages or delayed deliveries, our drones can fly up to 112km/h (depending on load). Our cutting-edge technology is here to truly revolutionise your business!

Founded in 2021 in Warsaw, our mission was to help deliver crucial medical goods during the pandemic in an innovative, safe, and efficient way. Shortly afterwards, we began cooperating with various businesses across Warsaw and our growth expanded. We started delivering a variety of goods ranging from computer microchips to coffee.
